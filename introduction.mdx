## What is Polychads?

Polychads is an insider trading detection system for [Polymarket](https://polymarket.com). We monitor on-chain activity and detect suspicious patterns — **fresh wallets**, **accumulators**, and **coordinated clusters** — and deliver alerts in real-time.
