## Scoring Factors

The signal score is calculated from:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Wallet Age" icon="clock">
    Newer wallets score higher. Fresh wallets created within days of trading are the strongest signal.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Trade History" icon="chart-line">
    Fewer previous trades = higher score. One-market wallets are suspicious.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Position Size" icon="dollar-sign">
    Larger USDC positions increase the score. Small "test" trades score lower.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Price Timing" icon="crosshairs">
    Buying at extreme odds (very low or very high) when the market later moves significantly.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Usage Tips

<Tip>
**For trading signals**, filter for `min_signal=60` to focus on high-confidence alerts.

**For research**, include all scores to see the full picture of market activity.
</Tip>

### Filter by Signal in API

```bash
# Only high-signal alerts
curl -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_KEY" \
  "https://polychadsbot.xyz/api/v1/alerts?min_signal=70"
```
